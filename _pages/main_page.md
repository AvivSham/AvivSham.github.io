I hold a PhD in Machine Learning from Bar Ilan University, where I worked at ChechikLab under the supervision of <a href="https://chechiklab.biu.ac.il/~gal/"> Prof. Gal Chechik</a>, head of the <a href="http://chechiklab.biu.ac.il/"> Computational Neurobiology Lab</a> & Nvidia Research Group IL. My research focuses on multi-task learning, learning in weight-space methods, and generative AI for speech.

Currently, I work as Data Science Tech Lead at <a href="https://www.aiola.com/">aiOla</a>, where I lead the technical design and architecture of data science projects, driving research through publications in top-tier conferences.
